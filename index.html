About Brahmakumaris

The Brahma Kumaris is a worldwide spiritual movement dedicated to personal transformation and world renewal. Founded in 1936 in Hyderabad, Sindh (now part of Pakistan) by Prajapita Brahma Baba, the organization has grown to encompass millions of students in over 100 countries.

Our Durg, Chhattisgarh center serves as a spiritual hub for the region, offering courses, meditation sessions, and spiritual guidance to all who seek inner peace and self-realization.

Through the practice of Rajyoga meditation, we help individuals connect with their inner selves and the Supreme Soul, leading to a life of peace, purity, and purpose.

The Real God Concept

Understanding God Shiva - The Supreme Soul

According to Brahmakumaris philosophy, God Shiva is not a physical being but the Supreme Soul, a point of conscious light energy residing in the supreme region beyond the physical world.

  • God Shiva is the incorporeal, eternal source of all spiritual knowledge and power
  • He is the Ocean of Peace, Purity, Love, Happiness, and Knowledge
  • God Shiva never takes birth in a physical body but descends into the body of Brahma Baba to impart divine knowledge
  • The true form of God is a tiny point of light, not the human-like forms often depicted
  • God Shiva is the creator of the new world order (Golden Age) through the transformation of human souls
  • He is the one who liberates all souls from sorrow and bestows salvation

Through Rajyoga meditation, we can establish a direct connection with God Shiva, experiencing His divine qualities and receiving His guidance for our spiritual journey.
